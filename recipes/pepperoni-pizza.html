Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This pepperoni pizza recipe produces a quick and easy classic! Delicious homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce have never been easier. Here are the ingredients:

  • 1 pount ground beef
  • 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked egg noodles
  • 20 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 20 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce, or as needed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sliced pepperoni, coarsely chopped
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
  • 1 (2.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Add ground beef to a skillet over medium heat; cook until no longer pink, breaking beef apart into crumbles as it cooks, about 10 minutes.
  3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 5 minutes; drain.
  4. Combine beef, noodles, 1/2 mozarella cheese, spaghetti sauce, pepperoni, black olives, and mushrooms in a large bowl; transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish. Top with remaining 1/2 cheese. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbling, 30 to 45 minutes.
